About Us

Sol y Mar Hotel is an eco-conscious retreat nestled between the ocean and tropical forest. Designed for travelers who want to unplug from technology and reconnect with nature, our hotel offers sustainable luxury in a serene coastal setting.

From solar-heated pools to farm-to-table dining, every detail reflects our commitment to sustainability. No Wi-Fi in rooms. No digital distractions. Just peace, sea breeze, and authentic experiences.

Restaurant & Dining

Our restaurant Mar y Tierra serves creative dishes made from ingredients grown in our garden or sourced from local fishermen and farmers.

  • Breakfast: Fresh fruit, house-baked bread, local honey
  • Lunch: Grilled fish tacos, tropical salads, coconut ceviche
  • Dinner: Tasting menu with wine pairing under the stars
  • Drinks: Organic cocktails, herbal infusions, craft beer

Tip: Ask about our beachfront dinner experience — a romantic setup with candles and ocean sounds.
